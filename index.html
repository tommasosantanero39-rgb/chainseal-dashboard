02 / Revenue Model

Subscription SaaS.
One flywheel.

Near-zero marginal costPaying Users × ARPU × 12
Revenue Formula
Revenue = Paying Users × ARPU × 12

A pure subscription model where recurring monthly plans drive predictable, scalable revenue — identical in logic to Spotify or Netflix. Creators and buyers subscribe to unlock platform capabilities; ChainSeal earns from subscriptions, not individual transactions.
